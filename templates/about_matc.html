About The Manually Annotated Twitter Corpus Project

What is The Manually Annotated Twitter Corpus Project?

The Manually Annotated Twitter Corpus Project (a.k.a. The MATC Project) is a crowdsourced part of speech tagging game that results in a corpus of words sourced from Twitter.

But what does that even mean?

Basically, there's two parts to The MATC Project: Tres Tigres, and The TwitUrban Dictionary.
