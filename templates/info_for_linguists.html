Information for Linguists (and Interested Non-Linguists)

How did you choose your part of speech tags?

I slightly modified the tags found in this paper from researchers at Carnegie Mellon University. In general, I chose to get rid of the tags in the paper that were described as 'Twitter/online-specific' and 'Miscellaneous'. I am interested in creating a corpus of text speak/neologisms that may not be annotated anywhere else (or available for free). Emoticons, punctuation, numerals, Twitter handles, et cetera, are not linguistically relevant for the purposes of The TwitUrban Dictionary.

If you have questions or comments about why I chose to only tag these certain parts of speech, please contact me.

For more information on the part of speech tags, please see the guide.
