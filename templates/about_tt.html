About Tres Tigres

What is Tres Tigres?

Tres Tigres is a crowdsourced part of speech tagging game.

How do I play?

If you haven't already, first you'll have to register and sign in.

Do I need linguistic training to play?

Nope! The whole idea behind The Manually Annotated Twitter Corpus Project is to allow linguistic data to be annotated by people with little or no training in linguistics. You don't need to know anything about morphemes or syntax; you just need to be a native or fluent speaker of English who enjoys MadLibsâ„¢!

What does 'Tres Tigres' mean? Why the name 'Tres Tigres'?

'Tres Tigres' means 'three tigers' in Spanish. As for why I chose it, I like tigers and tongue twisters. There's a fun tongue twister in Spanish about three tigers: 'Tres tristes tigres tragaban trigo en tres tristes trastos sentados tras un trigal. Sentados tras un trigal, en tres tristes trastos tragaban trigo tres tristes tigres.'

Also I like inside jokes.
