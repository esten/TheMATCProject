About Tres Tigres

What is Tres Tigres?

Tres Tigres is a crowdsourced part of speech tagging game.

How do I play?

You shall see...

Do I need linguistic training to play?

Nope! The whole idea behind The Manually Annotated Twitter Corpus Project is to allow linguistic data to be annotated by people with little or no training in linguistics. You don't need to know anything about morphemes or syntax; you just need to be a native or fluent speaker of English who enjoys MadLibs!
