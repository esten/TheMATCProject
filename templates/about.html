About The Manually Annotated Twitter Corpus Project

What is The MATC Project?

The Manually Annotated Twitter Corpus Project is a crowdsourced part of speech tagging game that results in a corpus of words sourced from Twitter.

But what does that even mean?

Basically, there's two parts to The MATC Project: Tres Tigres, and The TwitUrban Dictionary.

About Tres Tigres

What is Tres Tigres?

Tres Tigres is a crowdsourced part of speech tagging game.

How do I play?

Do I need linguistic training to play?

Nope! The whole idea behind The Manually Annotated Twitter Corpus Project is to allow linguistic data to be annotated by people with little or no training in linguistics. You don't need to know anything about morphemes or syntax; you just need to be a fluent or native speaker of English who enjoys MadLibs!

Why the name 'Tres Tigres'?

About The TwitUrban Dictionary

What is The TwitUrban Dictionary?

The TwitUrban Dictionary is a crowdsourced, manually annotated corpus of words pulled from Twitter. It is constantly updating.

You can browse The TwitUrban Dictionary online, or you can download it onto your own computer.

Do I need to sign up or pay to download it?

No. You don't need an account to access The TwitUrban Dictionary. Anyone can download The TwitUrban Dictionary, free of cost.
